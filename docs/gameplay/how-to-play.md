# How to Play Phalanx Duel

Phalanx Duel is a tactical card game where you deploy forces to a battlefield and launch strategic attacks to defeat your opponent.

## Objective
Reduce your opponent's **Lifepoints (LP)** from 20 to 0. You can also win if your opponent runs out of cards or exceeds the pass limit.

## Basic Rules
1. **The Board**: Each player has a battlefield with **Columns** and **Ranks**.
2. **Deployment**: Play cards from your hand onto your battlefield to build your defense and prepare for attacks.
3. **Attacking**: Use cards in your front rank to attack your opponent's columns.
4. **Suits & Effects**:
   - **Spades (♠)**: Double damage to the opponent player.
   - **Clubs (♣)**: Double damage to opponent cards (once per attack).
   - **Diamonds (♦)**: Shields adjacent cards, absorbing damage.
   - **Hearts (♥)**: Heals you or absorbs damage meant for you.

## Turn Structure
Each turn follows a strict sequence:
`Start` → `Deploy` → `Attack` → `Cleanup` → `Reinforce` → `Draw` → `End`
